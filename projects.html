This project was completed as part of a field session, a capstone course at my university. They requested that our team build a dashboard to visualize their inventory data and predict future inventory needs based on key factors.
To tackle this project, our team divided the work into three components: front-end development, back-end development, and machine learning. I was responsible for the machine learning component. I then developed a Random Forest regression model to predict future sales based on factors such as the time of year and the type of merchandise.
The final deliverable was a dashboard that displays the current inventory alongside predicted inventory levels for the next year, categorized by item. While the model was trained on just four months of data, its accuracy will improve as more data is added.
