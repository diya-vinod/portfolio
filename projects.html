Internship Project

During my internship at Verizon, I was tasked with building a tool for the network team that would help them determine project acceleration or deceleration based on the anticipated remaining amount left to commit and spend. I used the data in their project management tool. I first did some data preprocessing and created visualizations to gain a better understanding of the factors that contribute to the commit and spend of a project. I then trained and tested random forests regression model that predicted the commit and spend based on certain project factors. Then I experimented with different combinations of inputs to acheive the highest model accuracy. I validated the model and used it to calcuale the commit and spend on unseen data. This tool will help the network team make informed decisions about their budget and project timelines.
