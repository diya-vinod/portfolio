Field Session Project

Python Scikit-Learn Numpy Pandas Dash Google Colab

📌 Project Overview

💡 I collaborated with a team of three during a Field Session Capstone to assist our client, Rocky Mountain Lab Rescue, a nonprofit dedicated to rescuing and rehoming dogs.

🎯 The Challenge

  • The rescue heavily relies on donations generated from merchandise sales.
  • Inventory decisions were made intuitively, leading to stockouts & overstock.
  • The client requested that we build a dashboard to track sales trends & predict future inventory needs.

👥 The Team

  • We divided the dashboard into three components: front-end development, back-end development, and machine learning.
  • I was responsible for the machine learning component.

🤖 Machine Learning Model

  • I cleaned and processed inventory data for modeling.
  • I explored demand patterns using visualizations & EDA.
  • I built a Random Forest regression model to forecast future sales based on factors such as the time of year and the type of merchandise.
  • I trained the model to improve as more data becomes available.
  • We integrated predictions into an interactive dashboard.

🚀 Impact & Deployment

  • My team delivered a live dashboard showing current & predicted inventory levels.
  • We helped the nonprofit anticipate high-demand seasons and plan better.
  • Our client was very pleased with the dashboard! 🎉
